Pricing

Rates & fees

Labor $70/hr Time is billed from arrival until departure, rounded to the nearest minute. Time for Diagnosis not counted towards labor.
Drive-up $15 base · $2/mi after 15 mi From Carmen Drive and E Ponderosa Drive, Camarillo. One-way mileage.
Parts pickup $2 /mi To the nearest AutoZone for All Part Pickups. Miles only—no labor charge for the run.
Diagnosis $125 Fee waived when the repair is completed by us. Diagnosis is Flat Rate. Time for diagnosis not counted towards labor.

Common service costs

Typical labor estimates based on common job times. Expand for details.

Oil change
  • Conventional: ~0.5 hr labor + parts (filter & oil)
  • Synthetic: ~0.5 hr labor + parts (filter & oil)
Brake pads (front or rear)
  • Front or rear axle: ~1–1.5 hrs labor + parts
  • Both axles: ~2–2.5 hrs labor + parts
Battery replacement
  • ~0.5 hr labor + battery cost
Spark plugs
  • 4-cylinder: ~1 hr labor + parts
  • 6-cylinder: ~1–1.5 hrs labor + parts
  • 8-cylinder: ~1.5–2 hrs labor + parts
Air filter (engine cabin)
  • Engine air filter: ~0.25 hr labor + parts
  • Cabin air filter: ~0.25–0.5 hr labor + parts
Coolant flush
  • ~1 hr labor + coolant
Tire rotation
  • ~0.5 hr labor

Prices may vary depending on vehicle make, model, and condition. All estimates are subject to inspection and approval before work begins.
